As you embark upon your mission to explore this world and repair your ship.
Here are some basic commands:

Navigation:
    Use cardinal directions to explore. (e.g., "Go North")
    Type "Look" to observe your surroundings.
    Type "Search"

Interaction:
    To examine items or creatures, use "Examine"
    Collect items with "Take" or "Get"
    Engage in dialogue with creatures using "Talk"

Commands:
    For a list of available commands, type "Help" at any time.
